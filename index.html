Technical Architecture and Information Data Security

Ultra-Low Latency Optimization

Focuses on LLM inference architecture optimization to ensure ultra-low voice dialogue latency. This eliminates the awkward pauses typical of traditional voice systems, achieving real-time, fluid communication comparable to human interaction.

Healthcare Data Privacy Protection

Supports local inference and closed Vector DB (RAG), offering enterprise-grade on-premise deployment or private cloud solutions. This ensures sensitive Hospital Authority (HA) and patient medical data never leaves the territory, 100% complying with Hong Kong healthcare regulatory and privacy requirements.

Seamless Technical Integration Capabilities

Pureverse provides enterprise-grade integration suites (SDK/API) combined with Tone of Voice Analysis, without altering Sonne Technology's existing system architecture.

Low Cost, High Efficiency

Precisely controls API transaction costs and computing resources, ensuring exceptional cost-effectiveness even during large-scale, territory-wide hospital deployments in Hong Kong.

Business Model and a Win-Win Strategy

Transforming AI into a Driver for Sustainable Growth

Hardware Licensing

Directly integrates the AI voice module into Sonne Technology's hardware, such as automated dispensing kiosks and smart beds, charging a one-time Setup Fee per device alongside an annual software Maintenance Fee.

SaaS Subscription

For medication Q&A hotlines and automated outbound reminders, charges are billed per call minute (Pay-as-you-go) or as a stable monthly subscription fee, creating recurring revenue for hardware equipment providers.

Premium Upsell

Upsells Voice Agent upgrade packages (Software Upgrades) to Sonne Technology's existing customer base who have already purchased medical equipment, reactivating current clients and boosting Customer Lifetime Value (LTV).
