Sonne Smart Closed-loop Medication Management System

Technical Architecture & Information Security

Ultra-low Latency Optimization

Focused on LLM inference architecture optimization to ensure extremely low voice conversation latency. Eliminates the pausing sensation of traditional voice systems, achieving real-time, fluent communication comparable to human interaction.

Medical Data Privacy Protection

Supports Local Inference and closed-loop Vector DB (RAG), providing enterprise-grade On-Premise deployment or private cloud solutions. Ensures sensitive medical data of the Hospital Authority and patients does not leave the region, fully compliant with Hong Kong healthcare regulations and privacy requirements.

Seamless Technical Integration

Pureverse provides enterprise-grade integration suites (SDK/API), combined with Tone of Voice Analysis, without altering Sonne Technology's original system architecture.

Low Cost & High Performance

Precisely controls API transaction costs and computational resources, ensuring extremely high cost-effectiveness even during large-scale, hospital-wide deployments across Hong Kong.
